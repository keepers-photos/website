Keepers: Photo Journal for Busy Parents

What can Keepers do for you?
Newborn parents take more than 20,000 photos in the first year. In a world that moves ever faster, how do you keep up?
3-minutes every day is all you need to build a legacy of memories. Keepers is a free app that helps you tell your family's stories, so your children will grow up with a robust sense of self, to understand who they are and where they come from.
Keepers is designed by parents for parents. We understand how important privacy is to you. All your photos remain on your device.
Let Keepers takes care of the repetitive and tedious tasks, so you can spend your precious time on what technology can never replicate â€“ the intimate stories of your family, told in your own voice.
How Keepers Works.
Keepers filters out screenshots, de-duplicates and picks the best photos from what you took.
Smart face-detection ensures everyone looks their absolute best in every photo.
You're the curator of your own story - making changes is as easy as drag-and-drop.
Browse and preview beautiful templates to find your perfect page layout instantly.
3 minutes each day is all it takes - your beautiful book builds itself over time.
Give each book its own personality with a custom cover that celebrates your family's story.
